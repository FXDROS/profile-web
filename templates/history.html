Yes, this is one of my favorite since I finally liberated from the 6—16 prison. Well, not really a prison. It's just that high school really drained my energy.



Student Exchange to Japan

こんにちは！That's one of Japanese vocabulary that I remember the most; it means "hello" hahaha. Well, at 2017 I applied to participate in a student exchange program in Japan. It is an annual event held between my high school and Haruhigaoka High School in Nagoya, Aichi. It was really an unforgetable moment since I was able to improve my Japanese skill and made lots of friends.



Student Immersions Programme to Australia

This is the first time for me to participate in a students mmersion or student exchange programme. I tried to apply for this event that held by my school with collaboration with St.Paul's School Australia. This event is organized by Adonta Education and held for two weeks.



Participate In International Ursuline Youth Day

This is one of my favorite event that I've ever participated in. In this event, Ursuline's students from all over the world gathered together. There are students from Japan, Taiwan, Thailand, Indonesia, and others. In 2015, this event held in Surakarta, Central Java.



Vice President of the Student Council

I've never think that I could held this position when I just enter junior high school in St. Ursula BSD. Lot's of my friends supported me, but some didn't. First, I imagine that I would be super busy with all of those organization stuffs. But it wasn't as bad as I imagine. Working in a team taught me a lot; that I wasn't alone. I have my team, my friends, and the president herself. I was able to work pretty well because of their help, I suppose.

2010—2014

No Longer An Elementary

Finally graduated from elementary school after these tiring six years of my life. Kinda glad, kinda proud, but also sad. I didn't sad because of my grades (means they are fine) but because of my friends. It feels like a farewell for me and some of my friends. Those moments we cherished together, I won't forget that.



Let's Make Some Noises

There was a thing that made my elementary moments are amusing. Once, we were in a middle of a test. I was humming quietly but suddenly I got my voice lost of my control. I was suspected but of course, I denied it. Since that time, I never ever humming in any kind of test.



Back To Kindergarten

There was a storytelling competition when I was a third grader. I was one of the representative from my class. We won and got the second place. It didn't end just like that. The headmistress asked us to perform in front of kindergartens. It was a pleasure for us, and we did it pretty well.
